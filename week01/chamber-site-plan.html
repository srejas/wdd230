Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Spencer Rejas

Site Name

Daybreak Chamber of Commerce. Short and to the point, this name indicates the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Daybreak Chamber of Commerce will be the digital platform by which the businesses in the chamber can market and promote their local businesses, providing information that interested parties would find helpful. The visits to the site will directly correlate with the growth of the businesses and will help to grow the community economy.

Scenarios

What can I do to get involved with/join the chamber?

Where and when is the next business networking event?

What can I do to reach more customers of varying demographics?

Color Schema

The colors seen below will be used throughout the site as follows:

Silver, CBCBCD - primary color. This color will be used for the majority of backgrounds throughout the site.

Gunmetal, 2A343C - secondary color. This color will be used for the font and the background of the navigation.

Avocado, 457604 - accent color. This color will also be used as an accent color throughout the site.

Lapis lazuli, 005C7A - accent color. This color will also be used as an accent color throughout the site.
